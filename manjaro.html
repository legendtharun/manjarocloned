MANJARO EMPOWERING DEVICES AND USERS


Manjaro is a free and open source Linux operating system that emphasizes user privacy and control of their hardware. It is user-friendly and suitable for those new to computers, and can be installed on a wide range of devices. Architectures supported include x86-64 and ARM, making it a great choice for any device or computing environment. Manjaro provides numerous customization options and applications, as well as security and privacy features. It also offers several graphical user interfaces.

DO ANY TASK IN MANJARO

Open source
Choose a desktop user interface to create your ideal computing environment. With Manjaro, you can easily install a plethora of Open Source software, utilizing either native packages, Flatpak or Snap Packages. Additionally, you can take advantage of the Arch User Repository to build your own customized packages. Manjaro provides the ultimate flexibility and control over your computing experience.
Compatibility
Boost older hardware or fully harness the capabilities of the latest hardware. Manjaro is powered by the robust Linux Kernel and supports touchscreens, sound cards, graphics cards, and peripherals out of the box, without the need to search for Drivers from manufacturers. This means you can enjoy a hassle-free experience with Manjaro, focusing on your work or leisure activities rather than dealing with tedious driver installations.
Office
Effortlessly tackle any office task with the comprehensive office suites available. These suites support multiple document formats and adhere to document format standards, ensuring compatibility and ease of use. In addition, Manjaro offers a range of email clients, allowing you to stay on top of your email inbox and communication needs. Stay organized, productive, and efficient.
Gaming
Seamless gaming experience with the option to play native games using Steam or Lutris. You can also emulate other game platforms with Steam Proton or Wine. Retro gaming enthusiasts can enjoy playing their favorite classic games using the various retro emulators available on the platform. Simplified media consumption with the ability to play media or video content, and even set up a dedicated media center.
Creativity
Unleash your creativity and bring your design projects to life. Design graphics and vector art using Inkscape or GIMP, or create 3D models with Blender or FreeCAD. When you're ready to print your 3D designs, use Prusa Slicer to simplify the process. You can also edit your videos using popular software options like KDE-Live or OpenShot. For content creators Open Broadcast Software (OBS) for recording and streaming videos. Take your designs and video content to the next level.
DevOps
Take full control of your projects by utilizing the powerful git version control system. In addition, the platform offers robust virtualization capabilities, enabling you to deploy and run virtual machines with ease. Simplified containerization with the ability to manage Docker or Kubernetes instances. You can remotely access, configure, and administer your machines via various protocols such as SSH, VNC, RDP, or Telnet, giving you unparalleled flexibility and control over your computing environment.
Development
Manjaro offers a versatile platform for software development with support for many programming language. You can build software and user interfaces using your preferred programming language and test on the latest software with a rolling release model. The platform also provides access to a range of popular text editors, including Visual Studio Code, PyCharm, and Sublime Text. For more complex development projects, you can run a comprehensive integrated development environment (IDE) such as Eclipse or Apache NetBeans. Bring your software development projects to life.
Communication
Stay connected with your team in real-time using a variety of communication tools. Conduct chat or video meetings seamlessly using Mattermost, Slack, Telegram. The platform offers a vast selection of communication tools to suit your team's specific needs. Easily collaborate and communicate with your team.

Manjaro Linux Open Source operating system is and always will be provided for you to download use or modify free of charge and completely open source.
